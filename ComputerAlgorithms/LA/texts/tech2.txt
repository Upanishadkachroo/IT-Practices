Apex Robotics unveiled its next-generation domestic helper drone, the 'Aura 3.0,' boasting improved battery life and a new spatial awareness chip. The company claims the drone can navigate complex indoor environments with 99.9% accuracy and handle delicate tasks like sorting laundry and fetching small items. Pre-orders start next week, with the basic model priced at $1,200. The announcement signals a significant jump in consumer accessibility for autonomous smart devices.
